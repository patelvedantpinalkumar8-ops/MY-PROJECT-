# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **professional equity research report** on **NCC Ltd**, covering business fundamentals, financial statement analysis, valuation, competitive positioning, order book analysis, and investment recommendation.

The objective was to simulate the research process followed by equity research analysts and investment banking professionals by combining financial analysis with AI-assisted research.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

✔ Understand the company's business model

✔ Analyze historical financial performance

✔ Study profitability, liquidity and efficiency

✔ Evaluate order book strength

✔ Perform relative and intrinsic valuation

✔ Compare NCC Ltd with industry peers

✔ Identify business risks and opportunities

✔ Arrive at an investment recommendation
